Game Jam Research

My research into the impact of incentives and competition on game jam participation and behaviour, as presented at the International Conference of Game Jams, Hackathons and Game Creation Events 2023 and subsequently published. My findings suggest that incentive contingency is the key to effectively using incentives to inspire greater creativity in game design processes.
