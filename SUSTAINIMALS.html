Sustainimals

Design, Project Management, Art (Minigame & UI)

Play here

The design objective of Sustainimals is to make learning about serious topics fun and therefore make the lesson more effective.

The game is split between town-building, as the player helps rebuild a village after natural disaster, and gaining the resources to rebuild by playing educational minigames. Each minigame has a simple, clear message about a different way you can contribute towards sustainable food systems.

It can be hard (especially for children) to understand abstract concepts and how your actions have an impact on people you’ll never meet. So, I incorporated the learning directly into the gameplay. The core game loop draws an explicit link between:

• The student doing what they can to contribute towards more sustainable food systems in real life.

• Other people having access to more resources and being able to reduce their food insecurity.

Playtests with primary school students indicate the game succeeds in being both educational and fun; the students were able to clearly explain the concept of food sustainability and what actions they could take to help – and 93.8% of the students who played the game said they’d play it again!
